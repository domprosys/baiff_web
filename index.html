mission >_<

The first international AI film festival in Romania co-organized by humans and AI agents alike. A revolutionary experience for both tech enthusiasts and new cinema creatives.

Step into the future of cinema at BAIFF, a groundbreaking celebration of creativity where human imagination meets the power of artificial intelligence. This unique event showcases short films, animations, and experimental narratives created entirely or in collaboration with AI tools — from scriptwriting and visual effects to sound design and direction.

Join us for thought-provoking screenings, panel discussions with filmmakers, AI researchers, and artists, and hands-on workshops that explore how artificial intelligence is reshaping the art of storytelling. Whether you're a filmmaker, a tech enthusiast, or simply curious about the evolving relationship between humans and machines, this festival offers a front-row seat to the next chapter in cinematic innovation.

interact >_+

Get closer to the machine.

Explore a rich, immersive AI presence through interactive installations, conversational agents, and hands-on experiments. This is where the audience doesn't just observe — it engages, prompts, and collaborates.

Human and AI hands reaching toward each other

what you'll find

AI Presence

From an AI jury that watches the competition to avatars that debate film and art, generative models are woven into the very fabric of the festival. Talk to them. Argue with them. See what they notice.

Try the Tools

Dedicated terminals let you test the same generative video and text tools used by the artists. Prompt an image, remix a clip, or simulate a jury of your own. No experience needed — just curiosity.

experience >_^

A new kind of show.

Live moments made possible by generative AI - where creativity meets code, and performance is co-written with machines.

featured moments

Prompt Battle

An original live format launched at BAIFF. Prompt wizards go head-to-head in a knockout tournament, generating visuals in real time from surprise themes inspired by cinema. The audience decides who advances - while livewire hosts keep the heat on.

Live AV Sets

Audiovisual performances powered by generative models. Neural visuals, synthetic textures, and pulsing beats - this is what happens when artists perform through AI.

workshop >_°

Learn from pioneering creators and join discussions on the future of AI in cinema.

Step into the future of filmmaking with Cinematic Futures, a groundbreaking workshop series that bridges the timeless craft of cinematic storytelling with the emerging possibilities of AI-generated content. Designed for filmmakers, artists, writers, and creatives of all levels, this series explores how artificial intelligence can enhance, challenge, and reshape the art of cinema.

Through hands-on sessions, thought-provoking discussions, and collaborative exercises, participants will:

  • [||||]Discover AI tools used in scriptwriting, storyboarding, visual effects and editing
  • Analyze iconic films and reimagine scenes using AI-generated imagery[||||]
  • [||||]Learn how to blend human creativity with machine-generated ideas for storytelling innovation
  • Debate the ethical, artistic, and practical implications of using AI in filmmaking[||||]
